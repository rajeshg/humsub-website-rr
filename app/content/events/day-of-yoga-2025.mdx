Observed on June 21, International Yoga Day honors the physical and spiritual strength that yoga contributes to the global community. While yoga serves as a valuable form of exercise and wellness for many, it also offers a daily practice that millions embrace. For countless individuals, it is a timeless way to harmonize the body, mind, and soul.
